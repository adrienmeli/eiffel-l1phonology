2.3 Fundamental chart

<e> pet Pete her here
<i> sit site fir fire
<o> not note or ore

  • Note that “<>” refers to the orthographic vowel (and spelling in general)

2.4 Recap

Sounds to master:

  • all consonants

  • 3 lax vowels:

    • /æ/
    • /e/
    • /ɪ/

  • in long vowels and diphthongs:

    • /aɪ /
    • /aʊ/
    • /əʊ/
    • /iː/
    • /uː/
    • /eɪ /
