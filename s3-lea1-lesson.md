## Recap

Sounds to master:

* all consonants

* 3 lax vowels: 
  - /\textipa{\ae }/
  - /\textipa{e}/
  - /\textipa{I}/

* in long vowels and diphthongs: 
  - /\textipa{aI }/
  - /\textipa{aU}/
  - /\textipa{@U}/
  - /\textipa{i:}/
  - /\textipa{u:}/
  - /\textipa{eI }/
